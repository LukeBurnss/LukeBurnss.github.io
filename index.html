LUKE BURNS - PROGRAMMER PORTFOLIO

Pen & Sword

Pen & Sword is a top down 2D hack n' slash meets tower defence style game developed using the Unity 2D game engine. I was one of three programmers in a team of eight, and focused on mechanics such as player and enemy movement and combat, as well as environmental interactions. This project is a demo submitted for the DES315 module in my third year at Abertay University.

Unreal Engine Gameplay Mechanic Project

For my gameplay mechanic project for the CMP302 module at Abertay, I created a basic 'wallrunning' mechanic showcased via a short parkour level, as well as featuring other environmental obstacles: water which slows the player down when walking in it, and gas which damages the player whilst they are within the hitbox.
